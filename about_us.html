About Us

Who We Are

At Alfa Modular Box, we pride ourselves on being a leading manufacturer of high-quality electrical modular boxes. With years of experience in the industry, we have earned a reputation for delivering products that meet the highest standards of durability, safety, and performance.

Our modular boxes are crafted from superior GI (Galvanized Iron) Sheets and are available in both 18 Gauge and 20 Gauge, ensuring you get the strength and reliability you need for any project. We offer a wide range of sizes, including:

  • 3x3" (2 Module)
  • 4x3" (3 Module)
  • 5x3" (4 Module)
  • 8x3" (6 Module)
  • 5x5" (8 Module)
  • 9x3" (8 Module)
  • 8x6" (12 Module)
  • 8x8" (18 Module)

Whether you're working on a small residential renovation or a large-scale commercial project, we have the perfect modular box to suit your needs.
